Welcome

Thanks for checking out my site! Please take a look around. You'll find a little information about myself, my blog, and some projects I worked hard to create.

That didn't quite work out, but I did manage to make a pretty cool little dog running company called Dog Jog in Peoria, IL. Before that, I studied and worked as a mechanical engineer at Purdue University and for Siemens. You can find more information about projects I worked on and more at the Projects button above.
